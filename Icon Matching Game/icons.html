Welcome to The Matching Game!!!

The rules are simple:

There are four icons on the right
And five icons on the left
You have to compare both sides until you find the extra one
Once you find the extra icon, then you move on to the next level
If you choose an empty space or the wrong icon than the game ends and you'll have to start over again!
